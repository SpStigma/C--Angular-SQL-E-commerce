Cookie Policy

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device to remember your preferences and enhance your experience.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Strictly Necessary: Essential for site functionality (e.g. shopping cart).
  • Performance: Collect anonymous data on how visitors use the Site.
  • Functionality: Remember choices you make (language, region).
  • Marketing: Track browsing habits to deliver relevant ads.

3. Third-Party Cookies

We may allow third-parties (analytics, advertising partners) to set cookies on our Site. We do not control their use of cookies; please review their policies.

4. Managing Cookies

You can set your browser to refuse cookies or delete existing cookies. Note that disabling cookies may affect Site functionality.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy; the latest version will always be available at /cookies.
